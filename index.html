Theory of Fundamental Belief (TFB)

The Theory of Fundamental Belief (TFB) proposes that belief is not merely an opinion, emotion, or cultural construct, but a structural cognitive axis that organizes the way human beings perceive, interpret, and respond to reality.

In this framework, belief precedes conscious reasoning, influencing the attribution of meaning, decision-making, and behavioral patterns.

This repository and its GitHub Pages publication also function as a public proof of existence for the official website tfbtheory.com, connecting the domain to the DOI, Wikidata entry, and the author's ORCID record.
